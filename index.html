Welcome to collage

Welcome to Government Polytechnic Mirzapur. Government Polytechnic, Mirzapur, is a college that was started by the Government of Uttar Pradesh in 1964. They offer five different three-year courses in engineering fields like Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering (Production), Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, and Information Technology. Additionally, they introduced a new one-year course in "Data Science and Machine Learning" in 2022. In total, the college can admit 405 students across these courses at Government Polytechnic, Mirzapur. Government Polytechnic, Mirzapur is one of the oldest polytechnic institutes in Uttar Pradesh. They are affiliated with the Uttar Pradesh Board of Technical Education, Lucknow, and all the courses they offer are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Government Polytechnic, Mirzapur, has a strong academic history and is known for helping its students find good jobs with major companies in India. Admission to the Government Polytechnic, Mirzapur will be granted the Joint Entrance Exam (JEE) that is conducted by the Joint Entrance Examination Council, Lucknow, popularly known as the JEECUP exam.

Boy's Hostel
By Admin - 01 Jan 2023

A boys' hostel is a residential facility designed to accommodate male students or individuals, providing them with accommodation, amenities, and a supportive living environment.

About Boy's Hostel

Boys' hostels usually have a variety of room configurations to suit different preferences and budgets. These may include shared rooms, where two or more students share a room, or individual rooms for those who prefer more privacy. The rooms are typically equipped with basic furniture such as beds, desks, chairs, and storage units, allowing students to comfortably study and relax in their living space.
